What it does

A full shipping console, not a rate widget.

Rate shopping

Live rates from every carrier on your EasyPost account, sorted cheapest-first and tagged with the fastest option. Price-check a route from two postal codes before you have an address saved.

Labels your printer wants

Choose PNG, PDF, ZPL or EPL and the exact size — 4x6, 4x7, 4x8, 4x5 or 8.5x11 — so labels come out right on thermal stock or plain paper. The purchased label renders in-app.

International and customs

Guided customs declarations with itemised contents, plus a built-in Harmonized Tariff Schedule lookup against the U.S. International Trade Commission database.

Tracking that keeps up

Polling every five minutes as standard, or opt into real-time webhook push updates over a local tunnel for instant status changes.

Batch shipping

Import a CSV of recipients, validate it before anything is charged, then bulk-rate, bulk-buy and generate combined labels.

After the label

Refunds for unused labels, added insurance, carrier pickups, and insurance claims for lost or damaged parcels — all from the same window.

Your data stays yours

Address book, shipment history and reporting live in a local database on your machine. Nothing is sent anywhere except EasyPost.

50 languages

The full interface is translated into fifty languages, selectable in Settings.
